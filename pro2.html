Understanding the Box Model

Look at how the CSS properties change the physical footprint of these two identical divs.

I am the Padding Box

I have 40px of padding. Notice how much breathing room I have inside my border? But my total visible size on the screen has grown!

I am the Margin Box

I have 0px of padding, so my text is squished against the walls. However, I have a 50px margin on my top and left, pushing me away from everything else.
